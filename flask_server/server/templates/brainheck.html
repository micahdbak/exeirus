Say heck. It's heck. If you don't understand it, then it's brainheck.

Imagine an array of values, defaulting to zero.
You operate on a certain index, increasing or decreasing as necessary.
Each operation is relative to this index---an operation being a capital letter.
1. The number preceding an operation is how many times the operation is run.
2. L decrements the index, and R increments.
3. P prints what is at the index, and S scans a character into the index.
4. F sets a flag... But does nothing just yet.
5. J skips an operation.
6. E compares what is at the index with what is at the next index. If they are equal, then it will backtrack to the last flag.
